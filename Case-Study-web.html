About a Project

In an era where digital transformation is pivotal, our SaaS platform emerges as a revolutionary tool designed specifically for townships, including Newark, Irvington, and others. This platform was born out of a need to streamline the cumbersome and outdated processes of business licensing and permitting, which were previously mired in paperwork and inefficiency.

Our platform was conceived to address the complexities faced by businesses in obtaining various permits and licenses. The traditional method involved a tangle of paperwork, prolonged waiting periods, and a lack of transparency, creating significant hurdles for businesses of all types, from small eateries to large hospitals. 

The primary challenge was the overhaul of an antiquated system that was not only time-consuming but also prone to errors and delays. Businesses struggled to navigate this labyrinth, often grappling with uncertainty and frustration. The townships, on their part, were burdened with the manual management of applications and inspections, leading to operational inefficiencies and a backlog of applications. 

Our SaaS platform was designed with the following key objectives:

  • Digital Transformation: Transition from a manual, paper-based system to a fully digital, automated process.
  • User-Centric Design: Create an intuitive and accessible platform for both township officials and businesses.
  • Process Efficiency: Streamline and optimize the application, review, and approval processes for licenses and permits. 
  • Financial Integration: Implement a secure, integrated system for financial transactions, enhancing payment and payout processes. 
  • Scalability and Sustainability: Ensure the platform's adaptability to various townships and contribute to environmental sustainability through a paperless approach.

Automated Application and Document Retrieval

Our platform revolutionizes the application process by introducing an automated system that not only allows users to submit their applications online but also intelligently retrieves necessary documents using the business’s EIN number. This feature significantly reduces the time and effort required for businesses to gather and submit various permits and licenses. By automating document retrieval, we eliminate manual errors and ensure that the application is complete and accurate, streamlining the initial stages of the licensing process.
